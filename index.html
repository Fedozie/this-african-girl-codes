This African Girl

TAG Organics is a brand committed to providing natural organic products finely crafted in the traditional African household best suited for the Black African skin and hair. We are focused on providing natural beauty products that is healthy, natural, effective and affordable.

 

TAG Organics is a brand built in Africa, for Africans by Africans. We all here at, just like our founder belives that, The black heritage is as much in our skin and hair as it is in our history.

 

Our Ideology is simple - healthy, natural, effective and affordable.

Our Black Soap Products

The Fruity Brightening Black Soap

The Fruity Brightening Black Soap is a black soap made with a blend from these natural fruits such as pineapple, citrus, water melon, tomatoes, carrots, papaya are the active ingredients and does an excellent job of gently exfoliating the skin to reveal a brighter, healthier and glowing skin.

The Lightening Black soap

The Lightening Black Soap is a black soap that is enriched with a healthy percentage of natural lightening agents excellently suited for treating skin hyperpigmentation, skin discolouration and uneven skin tone. Some of these lightening agents include goat milk powder, bear berry extract popularly known as arbutin, mulberry extract, kojic acid, carrot extract, papaya extract, etc. All the ingredients used in formulatiing this recipe have been carefully studied and are added in percentages that are considered safe and effective for skin consumption.

The Herbal Black Soap

The Herbal Black soap is a black soap that is enriched with varieties of plants and herbs specifically for treating skin problems such as acne, eczema, ringworm, psoriases and rash. The plants and herbs infused in this soap have been carefully studied and selected and is found suitable and potent for healing skin disorders for all skin types without causing any harm to the skin. Some of these herbs include Neem, Saffron, Green-tea, Moringa, Aloevera, etc.

OUR PRODUCTS

THE BLACK SOAP RANGE

The Black soap range is a range of products that consist of products made with
the African Black soap popularly called"Ose Dudu" by the Yourbas,"Sabulu" by
the Hausa and "Ncha nkocha" by the Igbos. The Varieties of Black soap in this
range include three(3) different Black soaps that treat different skin disorders.
This Varieties include;

The Fruity Brightening Black Soap

The Fruity Brightening Black Soap is a black soap made with a blend from these
natural fruits such as pineapple, citrus, water melon, tomatoes, carrots papaya
e.t.c are the active ingredients and does an excellent job of gently exfoliating
the skin to reveal a brighter, healthier and glowing skin

The Lightening Black soap

The Lightening Black soap is a black soap that is enriched with a healthy
percentage of natural lightening agents excellently suited for treating skin
hyperpigmentation, skin discolouration and uneven skin tone. Some of these
lightening agents include Goat milk powder, bear berry extract popularly known
as arbutin, mulberry extract, kojic acid, carrot extract, papaya extract etc. All
the ingredients used in formulatiing this recipe have been carefully studied and
are added in percentages that are considered safe and effective for skin consumption.

The Herbal Black Soap

The Herbal Black soap is a black soap that is enriched with varieties
of plants and herbs specifically for treating skin problems such as acne,
eczema, ringworm, psoriases, rash etc. The plants and herbs infused in
this soap have been carefully studied and selected and is found suitable
and potent for healing skin disorders for all skin types without causing
any harm to the skin. Some of these herbs include Neem, Saffron, Green-tea,
Moringa, Aloevera etc.
